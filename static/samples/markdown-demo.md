# Markdown Formatting Demo

This sample demonstrates how QuickReader handles **Markdown** formatting in the preview panel while maintaining smooth reading flow.

## Text Styling

You can use **bold text** for emphasis and *italic text* for titles or foreign words. Combine them for ***bold italic*** when needed.

Inline `code` appears in the preview but is skipped during reading, keeping technical documents readable.

## Paragraphs and Flow

Long paragraphs work seamlessly. The application automatically paginates content for comfortable reading sessions. Each page contains roughly 250 words, creating natural stopping points without interrupting your flow.

Whether you're reading documentation, articles, or books, the RSVP technique helps you consume content faster while maintaining comprehension. The key is consistent practice and gradually increasing your speed as you become more comfortable.

## Conclusion

This demo shows that formatted documents remain fully readable. The preview panel displays rich formatting while the reader focuses on one word at a time. Try adjusting the preview panel width in settings to find your preferred layout.
